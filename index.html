Key Features

  • Shrimps

    Shrimps are small, decapod crustaceans found in both saltwater and freshwater habitats worldwide. They are highly sought after for their succulent meat and are a staple ingredient in various cuisines around the globe. At our business, we pride ourselves on providing high-quality, sustainably sourced shrimps to meet the demands of our customers. Our shrimps are carefully harvested and processed to ensure freshness and flavor, making them perfect for restaurants, seafood markets, and culinary enthusiasts alike. Whether you're looking for large, juicy prawns for grilling or tiny, sweet shrimp for salads and pasta dishes, we offer a diverse selection to suit your needs. Our commitment to sustainable practices means that you can enjoy our delicious shrimps with peace of mind, knowing that they are harvested responsibly and with respect for the environment. Experience the exceptional taste and quality of our shrimps and elevate your culinary creations with the finest seafood available.

  • Tahong

    "Tahong" refers to green mussels, a type of shellfish commonly found in coastal waters. These mussels are known for their tender, flavorful meat and are a popular ingredient in various cuisines worldwide. Tahong are prized for their versatility and nutritional value, being rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
